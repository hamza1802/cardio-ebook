Weekly Schedule

Week 1-4

  • Monday: 30 min Jog or Cycle
  • Tuesday: 20 min HIIT (30s work / 60s rest)
  • Wednesday: Rest or Yoga
  • Thursday: 30 min Jog or Cycle
  • Friday: 45 min Brisk Walk or Hike
  • Saturday: Active Fun (Sport, Dance, Swim)
  • Sunday: Rest
